A · Fleet on the bay
Wide action, performance energy
Full-bleed wide shot of a fleet — Optimists, 420s, or college boats — racing on Biscayne Bay. Real conditions, real heel angle, spray. Headline anchored bottom-left, subhead beneath. MediaLooping 6–8s video preferred; high-res still as fallback. ToneAspirational, performance, Mouratoglou-direct.
Why this The "premier" claim is most credible against a real pro-tier action shot. The fleet (vs. a single boat) signals breadth — multiple sailors, multiple programs — without saying it. Easiest to source from existing club race photography.

B · Sailor close-up
Personal, aspirational, named
Tight portrait — a young sailor at the helm, focused expression, water spray, gear visible. Headline overlaid on the dark side of the frame, subject pushed to opposite third. MediaHigh-res still; hold for 4s, then cycle through 3–4 sailor portraits at different ages. ToneAthlete-as-protagonist, the "next generation" made tangible.
Why this Mouratoglou's later sites do this — a single face, not a brochure shot. Makes the academy feel about people, which is exactly the new chapter's claim. Best paired with rotating subjects so it doesn't lock to one sailor's story.

C · Heritage / Now diptych
The 80-year arc, in one frame
Split hero, 50/50. Left: black-and-white archival photo of CGSC sailors (1950s–60s). Right: full-color modern sailor on the same boat type. Headline lives on the right (the "now" half), label "1946" on the left. MediaTwo stills, no video. Subtle parallax on scroll — the seam slowly opens. ToneMost distinctive. Tells the whole brand thesis above the fold.
Why this The subhead's heritage→ambition shape becomes the visual itself. Most ownable — no other sailing academy has this shot. Risk: needs a specific archival photo to exist, and both halves have to be perfect or it falls flat.

D · Bay-as-territory
The place itself is the hero
Drone shot of Biscayne Bay — Miami skyline in the upper third, white sails dotted across the water below. Slow horizontal pan or static. Headline bottom-left. MediaDrone video, 8–12s loop. Cinematic. ToneInstitutional, territorial, "this is the place."
Why this Makes "in Biscayne Bay" the proof of "premier" — the territory carries the claim. Most cinematic. Risk: human-energy is low; the academy could feel like a backdrop to real estate. Best if paired with a strong human shot in section 2.
