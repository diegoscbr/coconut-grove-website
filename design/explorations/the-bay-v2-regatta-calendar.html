B · Horizontal year strip A literal year axis with regattas pinned at their actual dates. Marquee events (Orange Bowl, Don Q) get rust pins; programs (Summer, Fall RWB) get navy. Each pin has a card with name + dates + 1-line context.
Why thisMost distinctive — actual calendar shape carries the "every season" claim visually. Pin colors create instant hierarchy (peak events vs. ongoing programs). Works as an anchor for "what's next?" CTA at the bottom.
RiskMore design-fragile than the grid; if pin spacing isn't tuned right it looks crowded. Mobile collapse is harder — strip needs horizontal scroll on small screens.
