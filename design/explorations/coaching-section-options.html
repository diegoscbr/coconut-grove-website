A · Director-led portrait + staff grid Maru gets a hero portrait, name, title, short bio. Below or beside her, a grid of named staff with roles. Mouratoglou's exact treatment for his coaching page.
Why thisMost personality-driven. Establishes Maru as the face of the academy without ambiguity. Visitors get a clear "who's running this" answer in 3 seconds.
RiskPersonality-cult fragility — if Maru leaves, the section needs a rewrite. Also leans into a coach-as-celebrity register some sailing culture pushes back against.

B · Methodology-first Lead with the coaching philosophy as a headline + 4 numbered principles. People appear as a small named credit at the bottom. Most institutional / durable.
Why thisBets that "premier" is proven by how we coach, not by who. Survives any staffing change. Makes the page about the academy, not the celebrity.
