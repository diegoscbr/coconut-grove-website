C · Conditions calendar A 12-month heatmap visualizing sailable conditions every week of the year. Cool blues for steady, dark navy for strong training, rust for peak race weeks. Closes the "no off-season" claim with evidence.
Why thisMost rigorous proof for "warmest training waters." Looks like a serious training program's data sheet, not a tourism brochure. Distinctive — no other sailing club site does this.
RiskRequires real climate / wind data sourced and verified. If the cells are decorative-only, sophisticated visitors will spot it. Production cost: a real chart needs real numbers.

Pick a treatment. B is the safest cinematic option (one image does the work). C is the most distinctive ("no off-season" with receipts). D is the most experiential. A is the most utilitarian — best if Visit/Contact is going to lean on the geography too.
