Where do you fit?

The CGSC pathway.

All three programs feed the same pathway: Discover → Develop → Race → Compete → Perform. Camps and Adult Sailing are entry points; the Race Team is the competitive ladder; the High Performance Center is the apex.

Wherever you start, the same coaches see you all the way up. No re-onboarding, no switching languages — the next rung is always one practice away.

The same coaches, every program

Continuity is the point.

We don't rotate coaches by program. The staff that runs Race Team also coaches the High Performance Center and the adult classes. Wherever you enter, you stay with the same people.
