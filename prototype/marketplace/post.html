Before you post

A few reminders.

  1. Sailing-related items only. Boats, sails, rigging, hardware, electronics, apparel, safety gear. Not a general for-sale board.
  2. Be honest about condition. "Good" is a real word; "Like new" should mean it. Buyers will inspect at the dock.
  3. Set a real price. "OBO" is fine, but anchor it to something. Browsing competitive listings on West Marine or eBay first never hurts.
  4. Take a few photos. Phone camera, natural light, multiple angles. Photos do most of the work.
  5. You're responsible for the sale. CGSC isn't a party. Meet at the dock, inspect the gear, exchange money or trade, done.
