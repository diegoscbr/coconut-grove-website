House rules

A bulletin board, not a marketplace platform.

CGSC Marketplace exists so members can pass gear to each other directly. No platform fee, no escrow, no shipping calculator. Transactions happen at the dock; we just make the listings searchable.

  1. Members only. Posting and contact require a current CGSC membership. Browsing on the public site is allowed, but contact info is hidden until you sign in.
  2. Sailing-related items. Boats, sails, rigging, electronics, apparel, safety gear. Not a general for-sale board.
  3. Listings expire after 60 days. Re-post if it hasn't sold; helps keep the dock current.
  4. Transactions are between members. CGSC is not a party to any sale. Inspect the gear at the dock before you pay.
