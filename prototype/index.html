About

Eighty years on Biscayne Bay.

Founded in 1946 by Marshall Pollard, Wirth Monroe, Frank Kerdyk, and Jack Engle — CGSC has been the training ground for South Florida sailors at every level for the better part of a century.

The Instructional Center is the next chapter. The same standards, deeper coaching, a broader ladder — from first sail to Olympic campaign.
